# Transitions — Impact

> **How to use:** Impact transitions mark key moments in the script.
> Used before revealing something important or shifting the tone.

---

## Impact Transition List

1. "Okay. Now here's the game-changer."
2. "And this is where everyone self-sabotages."
3. "And that's why I do it differently."
4. "But there's one detail that changes everything…"
5. "Now… look at this."
6. "I know, it seems small. It's not."
7. "If you got that, you've already won half the battle."
8. "And before you say 'yeah, but…'"
9. "Hold on. I thought it was weird too."
10. "The truth is…"
11. "What nobody tells you is…"
12. "And here comes the boring part that actually works."
13. "This isn't motivation. It's mechanics."
14. "Making sense so far?"
15. "Now I'm going to show you this in the most visual way possible."
16. "If you blink, you'll miss it."
17. "This next sentence is screenshot-worthy."
18. "Now hold this image in your head."
19. "And this is where the video gets dangerous."
20. "Now I'm going to be brutal for 5 seconds…"

---

## When to Use

- **Before revealing the solution** → "Okay. Now here's the game-changer."
- **Before confronting** → "And this is where everyone self-sabotages."
- **To build anticipation** → "But there's one detail that changes everything…"
- **To validate the viewer** → "If you got that, you've already won half the battle."
- **To preempt objection** → "And before you say 'yeah, but…'"

---

## Usage Tips

- Pair with a VPB (visual cut) to reinforce impact
- Don't use more than 2-3 per video—they lose power if overused
- Ideal for marking the transition between "problem" and "solution"
