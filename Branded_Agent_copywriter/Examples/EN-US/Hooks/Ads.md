# Hooks — Ads

> **How to use:** Ad hooks are direct and call out objections head-on.
> They work best when acknowledging the pain before offering the solution.

---

## Category: Calling Out Objections

1. "If you're trying to [result] and hitting a wall, it's because of this."
2. "What nobody tells you about [common method]…"
3. "Before you spend money on [thing], do this first."
4. "Most people quit because of this objection: [objection]."
5. "If you've tried [X] and failed, look at this."
6. "This is for those who want [result] without becoming a slave to [sacrifice]."
7. "If you don't have time, this is the only way."
8. "The truth about [trend] that nobody admits."
9. "What's blocking you isn't [thing]. It's [thing]."
10. "The objection is valid. So is the solution."

---

## Category: Direct Promise

11. "You don't need [expensive thing]. You need a process."
12. "I'll show you the shortest path between you and [result]."
13. "Want to stop [pain]? Start here."
14. "You're 1 adjustment away from [result]."
15. "The mistake that makes you throw effort in the trash."
16. "If you want an applicable method, not theory, pay attention."
17. "This saves [time] per week."
18. "I give you the script. You just record."
19. "You can do this with a phone. Seriously."
20. "I'll show you how to make [result] seem inevitable."

---

## Category: Profile Segmentation

21. "If you want [result], stop starting with [X]."
22. "You don't need motivation: you need a ready-made script."
23. "If you hate being on camera, even better."
24. "This works for [specific profile]."
25. "You're trying to learn the slow way."
26. "If you want to sell without sounding like a salesperson…"
27. "If you're just starting, this is the honest shortcut."
28. "If you already have an audience and aren't converting, this is for you."
29. "If you don't know what to post, no more excuses."
30. "Most people mess this up. You won't."

---

## Recommended Structure for Ads

```
HOOK (objection/pain acknowledged)
    ↓
AGITATION (why it hurts / consequences)
    ↓
SMART ALTERNATIVE (the solution/method)
    ↓
PRE-CTA (when fitting — intermediate engagement)
    ↓
FINAL CTA (specific action)
```

---

## Usage Notes

- **Ideal duration:** 15-45 seconds (ads need to be direct)
- **Tone:** Empathetic but no-nonsense
- **Goal:** Qualify audience + generate immediate action
- **Caution:** Don't promise the impossible—maintain credibility
- **Tip:** Use specific numbers when possible ("saves 3 hours per week")
