# Hooks — Infotainment

> **How to use:** Replace brackets `[X]` with niche-specific content.
> Infotainment hooks are direct, punchy, and promise immediate value.

---

## Category: Common Mistake / Diagnosis

1. "You're doing [X] the expensive way… and the worst part: you don't even know it."
2. "If you still believe [belief], you're paying a stupidity tax."
3. "The problem isn't [pain]. It's how you're trying to fix [pain]."
4. "If you have [symptom], you're probably making this mistake."
5. "3 signs you're [error] without realizing it."
6. "You're trying to [goal] with a 2018 strategy."
7. "You're addicted to [mistake] and calling it 'productivity.'"
8. "What you call 'lack of time' is sometimes just [diagnosis]."
9. "If [symptom], then your problem is [cause]."
10. "You're confusing [thing A] with [thing B]. And that's why it hurts."

---

## Usage Notes

- **Ideal duration:** Infotainment hooks work best in 30-60 second videos
- **Tone:** Direct, slightly provocative, but not aggressive
- **Goal:** Generate curiosity + immediate identification with the problem
- **Caution:** Don't overpromise—the content needs to deliver what the hook promised
