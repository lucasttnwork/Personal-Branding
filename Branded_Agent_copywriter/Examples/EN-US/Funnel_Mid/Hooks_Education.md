# Education Hooks - Middle of Funnel (EN-US)

## Overview

Hooks for the consideration phase, when the audience already knows Lucas Nogueira and is looking to understand how to solve their problem. The goal is to educate, build authority, and demonstrate expertise through the F.O.C.U.S. Method and Synchronous Conversion Architecture.

**Tone:** Teacher-like, detailed, generous in sharing knowledge
**Focus:** Methodology (F.O.C.U.S.), how it works, deep education
**Funnel proportion:** 30% of content

## When to Use

- **Educational carousels:** Hooks 1-10 (Secret) + Hooks 31-40 (How-To)
- **Long-form videos (3-5 min):** Hooks 11-20 (Before/After) with case studies
- **Lives:** Hooks 21-30 (Social Proof) with Q&A
- **Content series:** Combine categories across multiple posts
- **YouTube:** Hooks 1-10 (Secret) as video titles

### Ideal Contexts:
- Content explaining the F.O.C.U.S. methodology
- Detailed case studies
- Real website analyses (with permission)
- Tutorials and step-by-step guides
- Project behind-the-scenes

## Application Notes

1. **Secret hooks** create expectation - content MUST deliver real value
2. **Before/After hooks** work best with visuals (screenshots, metrics)
3. **Social Proof hooks** build authority - use specific data when possible
4. **How-To hooks** are the most educational - ideal for carousels and long videos
5. This stage is the "natural habitat" of the brand voice - can be more detailed and technical
6. Use brand terminology: F.O.C.U.S., Strategic Digital Dissonance, Synchronous Conversion Architecture
7. American audiences appreciate specific numbers and concrete examples
8. Balance education with entertainment - even detailed content should be engaging
