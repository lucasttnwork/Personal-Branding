## Why This Works (US Market)

- **Strategic Vulnerability:** "Makes me uncomfortable" creates authenticity
- **Serialized Curiosity:** "30 days" creates anticipation to follow along
- **Skin in the Game:** Applying to own business = proof of confidence
- **Powerful Question:** The rhetorical question about trust is impactful
- **Cliffhanger:** "Tomorrow: defining the ideal client" creates anticipation
- **Soft CTA:** "Save" is less committal than "comment"
- **Building in Public Trend:** Very resonant with US startup/founder culture
- **Accountability:** American audience values public accountability and transparency
