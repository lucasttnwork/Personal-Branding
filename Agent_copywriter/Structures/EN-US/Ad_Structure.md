# Structure: Ad

> Template for direct promotional content.
> Ideal for: launches, offers, lead magnets, products.

---

## Base Formula

```
OBJECTION / OBSTACLE (pain acknowledgment)
    ↓
SMART ALTERNATIVE (the solution)
    ↓
PRE-CTA (when fitting)
    ↓
DIRECT CTA (specific action)
```

---

## Suggested Timeline (30-45s)

| Time | Element | Description |
|------|---------|-------------|
| 0:00-0:05 | **Hook** | Call out the main objection |
| 0:06-0:12 | **Agitation** | Why it hurts / consequences |
| 0:13-0:25 | **Solution** | The alternative / what you offer |
| 0:26-0:35 | **Proof** | Result, number, or testimonial |
| 0:36-0:45 | **CTA** | Specific action + urgency |

---

## Types of Ads

### Pain-Focused Ad
```
"If you're [pain], stop everything."
    ↓
"The problem isn't [obvious cause]. It's [real cause]."
    ↓
"That's why I created [solution]."
    ↓
"[Number] people have already [result]."
    ↓
"[CTA with link/action]."
```

### Outcome-Focused Ad
```
"[Specific result] in [time]."
    ↓
"Without [sacrifice the audience hates]."
    ↓
"Using [method/tool]."
    ↓
"Works for [specific profile]."
    ↓
"[CTA]."
```

### Objection-Focused Ad
```
"I know you think [objection]."
    ↓
"But [counter-argument]."
    ↓
"That's why [solution works like this]."
    ↓
"[CTA]."
```

---

## Required Elements

### Hook (0:00-0:05)
- [ ] Grabs attention of the right audience
- [ ] Filters out non-ICP
- [ ] Acknowledges a real pain

### Body (0:06-0:25)
- [ ] Agitates the pain (consequences)
- [ ] Presents clear alternative
- [ ] Differentiates from common solutions

### Proof (0:26-0:35)
- [ ] Specific number
- [ ] Short testimonial
- [ ] Visual before/after

### CTA (0:36-0:45)
- [ ] Specific action ("click," "comment," "visit")
- [ ] Urgency (when applicable)
- [ ] Low friction (easy to execute)

---

## Ad CTAs

### External Link
- "Link in bio."
- "Click the link and grab yours."
- "Visit [link name] and see how it works."

### Lead Magnet
- "Comment [WORD] and I'll send it."
- "Save and DM me."
- "Drop your email in the comments."

### Scarcity
- "Last [X] spots."
- "Only until [date]."
- "For those who see this video first."

---

## Variations

### Ultra-Short Ad (15s)
```
Hook + pain [0:00-0:05]
Solution in 1 sentence [0:06-0:10]
Direct CTA [0:11-0:15]
```

### Long Ad (60s)
```
Hook [0:00-0:05]
Quick story [0:06-0:15]
Problem [0:16-0:25]
Solution [0:26-0:40]
Proof [0:41-0:50]
CTA + urgency [0:51-1:00]
```

---

## Final Checklist

- [ ] Hook qualifies the right audience?
- [ ] Pain is real and specific?
- [ ] Solution is clear and differentiated?
- [ ] Has proof (number, testimonial, result)?
- [ ] CTA has low friction?
- [ ] Urgency is real (not fake)?
